UX/UI desinger | Valorant player | Matcha enthusiant | Oilers fan

I’m a UX/UI designer who cares about how things feel to use. I like taking messy, complicated problems and slowly shaping them into something clear and simple. A lot of that comes from my background in art, I’ve always been drawn to details, composition, and the small touches that make something feel intentional. I’m especially interested in work that helps people in real ways, whether that’s navigating healthcare, understanding finances, or just making everyday digital experiences a little less frustrating. For me, good design isn’t just about how it looks, it’s about making things easier and more approachable.
